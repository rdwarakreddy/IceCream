Where Sweet Moments Begin

From a small kitchen to a beloved brand, our journey has always been about crafting the perfect scoop with the ones we love.

Our Journey

Every flavor has a memory, and every scoop tells a story of passion and craftsmanship.

2015

The First Batch

Our journey started with a single hand-cranked churner in a small kitchen. Inspired by our grandmother's recipes, we experimented with local honey and wild berries.

storefront

2017

Our First Pop-up

We took our cart to the local farmer's market. Seeing the smiles of our first customers confirmed our belief: people crave authentic, artisanal treats.

2020

The Flagship Store

Despite the challenges, we opened our dream shop in the heart of the city. A place designed for gathering, laughing, and slow-melting afternoons.
